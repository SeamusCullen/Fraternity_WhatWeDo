What We Do

Our mission is to save children's lives

Fraternity Without Borders helps communities in the poorest places in the world, where hunger, misery and homelessness take new victims every day.

WE PROTECT: We help protect orphans, children and adolescents who are at high risk due to extreme poverty and disease.

WE PROVIDE: We help provide wells for clean water and help build centres for safety, community, education and health.

WE ENCOURAGE: We work with communities and encourage volunteering, solidarity and fraternity.

WE PROMOTE: We actively promote economic and social development, cultural expression and partnership to overcome poverty.

"Unclean water is an immeasurably greater threat to human security than violent conflict." (UN Development Programme report)

A well can provide clean water for a community of over 2,000 people.
So far, we have built 9 wells.

Our goal is to build 13 more. With your support, we can help 26,000 more people.

Community development

Fraternity Without Borders has built 26 community centres in Mozambique where children have access to food, health care, clean water, culture and education.

More than 9,000 vulnerable children and older people are being helped already.

Medical and dental missions are organized twice a year for examinations, treatments and emergency care.

Toilets are key investments in our community centres to promote good hygiene habits and prevent disease.

209,000 meals are provided every month

Sustainable development

Our community centres utilize local workforce, techniques and materials.

Some learning centres have already been provided with solar panels and with cisterns that collect rain and safeguard underground water.

212 members of staff from the community are working for the community.

Our pilot bakery currently employs the youngsters who were once assisted by the charity themselves.
